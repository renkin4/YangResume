Interests

兴趣

I love reading books, listening to musics and playing games. I spend most of my free time either creating new projects or when I am on a slump I will read some books like rich dad poor dad, or some technical books like gang of four, game engine architecture, etc.

Reading give me inspiration and motivation while playing games is what I normally and always love to do. That's why my first few jobs I looks for some game company to learn more about games from creating them and also got a degree in Games Technology.

I am also a nerd/geek when it comes to technology. I will spend month reasearching on something just to do undertanding it. And will feel a very satisfying sense of accomplishment when I finished understanding or create a project from those theories.

我十分热衷于电子游戏，不论是开发还是游玩。除去开发游戏的时间，我也会拿出很多空余的时间对当前的新游戏进行探索。最近我尝试了许多FPS游戏，对于一个FPS/RTS游戏开发者来说确实是很奇怪，因为即使我开发FPS/RTS游戏，我还是能对FPS游戏保持我的热情以及耐心，并且在不同的游戏中获得启发以提高自身的见解。
