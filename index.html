I found joy in developing games, a joy that's hard to explain in words. I especially love making multiplayer games. The feeling of connecting people together to experience the world I created is a weird but oddly satisfying feeling. As such, I have dedicated most of my Game Development career to research and improve on my skills in that particular department.

I always had dreamt even before my Game Development career. I dream of making a MMO game, a world which my team & I have carefully crafted and moulded. It's a big dream and there's a long road ahead but I will be persistent and continue to walk this road towards my goal.

我会在游戏开发中发现乐趣，那是一种无法言喻的快乐。我尤其喜欢制作多人游戏。创造一个可用把人们连接到一起的世界给了我极大的满足感。因此，我游戏开发经历中的大多数时间都在研究和改善我这方面的技术。我还没有进入游戏开发行业的时候，我常常梦想。

梦想可以开发一款MMO游戏，一个我和我的队友们精心雕琢的世界。这是一个很遥远的梦想并且前方的路很艰难，但是我会在我追梦的道路上不断前进。
